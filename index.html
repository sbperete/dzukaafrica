What We Do

Dzuka Africa StartUp Hub is an On the Job training and Incubation for Emerging Entrepreneurs, focusing on Business training, Co-working space provision and access to working tools or equipment to ensure success of Emerging Entrepreneurs in Blantyre,Malawi


OUR PROGRAMS

StartMeUp Program

Start Up Support for emerging Entrepreneurs with incubation, business training, access to working space and working tools or equipment.


Me2ICount

In pursuit to build sustainable communities. 80%of our Malawian school going population attend CDSS Community Day Secondary School , less than 5% of them will advance to tertiary level and the rest will be challenged due to many factors but not limited to, access to funds to advance their education, high drop out rate, lower points earned in exams. We at Dzuka Africa we have realized that every soul counts and each one has full potential. Me2ICount focuses on imparting social entrepreneurship learning to scholars teaching them how to create billable solutions for identified community challenges.

ABOUT US

Non-profit organization based in Blantyre, Malawi, our main focus is on empowering youth and Women with life and professional skills to enable them to be employable for entry level jobs and equally to be self-sustainable by building resilient Start-ups through Internship program, Entrepreneurship programs , Social Entrepreneurship programs and Mentorship program.
