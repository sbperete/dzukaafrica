Our Believe

he first 12 months Journey of any emerging entrepreneurs is the same, the emerging entrepreneurs despite their sector of choice they need business acumen and hand holding to increase their chances of success. Our Program is aimed at giving Start Ups and opportunity to be successful in their first two years.

What is START ME UP Program?

Start up and Development program for emerging entrepreneurs in the Southern Region of Malawi providing the follwoing support

  • Infrastructure support
  • Market access support
  • Mentorship support
  • Development and building of a reliable and trusted brands

Me2 I count

Community Day Secondary Schools, the reality of the matter. Most Scholars attending CDSS might not advance to tertiary school by the end of their secondary school enrollment due to various reasons it might be lack of finance, not attaining good grades to be accepted for tertiary education, not managing to pass etc. Dzuka Africa saw it fit to bring empowerment where it matters the most, Secondary School Entrepreneurship within communities we live in, this entailed training 190 Scholars per year about social entrepreneurship, from identifying real problems in society, selecting the challenge that can turn into income generating business, learning about financial management

Build Me up

Dzuka Africa works with women in business by connecting Governments and the private sector to encourage and support women’s economic empowerment. As well as doing trade within and across borders, the program give women a voice with policy makers to ensure that women entrepreneurship has a better environment and greater practices.
